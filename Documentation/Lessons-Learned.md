# Lessons Learned

## Authentication vs Authorization

Authentication answers:

Who are you?

Authorization answers:

What are you allowed to do?

---

## Azure Resource Tags

Tags organize Azure resources.

They do not provide security.

---

## Network Segmentation

Resources should be separated into logical subnets.

Segmentation improves:

- Security
- Performance
- Manageability

---

## Troubleshooting Principle

Always determine:

Who is affected?

Before attempting a solution.
